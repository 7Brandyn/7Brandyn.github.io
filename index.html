freevelo

Cross-Country Bicycle Touring Company

September 2022 - Present

Summary

After my bicycle trip across America, I became inspired to build a long-distance / cross-country cycling platform for those who on a budget.

Problem

Cross-country bicycling is an amazing way to explore, exercise, and make new memories...however, it doesn't come without it's roadblocks. After conducting short interviews with both avid cyclists and non-cyclists alike, I've found that there are 3 major reasons why so many bicycle-lovers are not engaging in long-distance bicycle travel:

#1 - "I dont have the time"
#2 - "I cant afford it"
#3 - "It doesn't seem safe"

I want to find ways to solve each of these problems, especially for the younger crowd.

Solution

#1

Time is more valuable than money, so I wanted to add a section in our "resources" tab where you can pick up cycling whenever its most convenient.

We also provide a list of "Mini-Tours" that might also include both free and paid options.
#2

Not everyone can afford a multi-day tour, so I wanted to give people the option to join free tours that can be sponsored by local bicycle shops and volunteers. Each tour has a recurring theme so that users would be able to plan ahead without having to check the site and possibly losing interest overtime.

#3

Safety is a HUGE concern for sure, so I wanted to make sure that we include videos on how to stay safe, what gear to bring, and the difficulty level of each tour.

Conclusion

I do not want to become just another bicycle touring site. I want Freevelo's mission to be to get everyone get involved towards a healthier, safe, and fun way of traveling.
